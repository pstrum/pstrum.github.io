Percentage Renewable Energy by State

Recently I wanted to explore the D3.js library. Big data is a BIG interest of mine, and I was intrigued by the possibilities of showing off various datasets. Also, for a recent project, I thought it might be helpful to explore the United States Energy Information Administration API.

Below is a little app that shows the total energy produced by each state (in yellow) and the total renewable portion of that energy (in green).

As far as the code goes, the <html> is what you see: a <select> tag for choosing a state and then an empty <svg> to hold the JavaScript generated chart utilizing D3.js.

For the JavaScript, I have a module that creates a new Promise object to utilize to make a get request. Then I have a module that parses the data returned, and then displays the data–which is the main D3.js logic. Check out these modules below.
