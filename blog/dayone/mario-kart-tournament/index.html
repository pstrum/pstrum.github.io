Mario Kart Tournament

How it works

  • 4 people play 4 courses
  • Each player gets to pick a course
  • Players record their points
  • ...next 4 players start a new round
  • Each player plays 3 rounds total (12 courses)

Rules

  • No CPU racers
  • All items available

Determine a winner

  • After the 3 rounds, total each player's points across all the rounds.
  • Top 4 players face off in a final round.
  • Tie for 4th place is determined with a Time Trial on a random course.

Other Stuff

Depending on how many people attend, we would shuffle up the players each round. For example, with 8 people:

First Round
1 vs 2 vs 3 vs 4, and 5 vs 6 vs 7 vs 8

Second Round
1 vs 2 vs 7 vs 8 and 3 vs 4 vs 5 vs 6

Third Round
1 vs 3 vs 5 vs 7 and 2 vs 4 vs 6 vs 8
