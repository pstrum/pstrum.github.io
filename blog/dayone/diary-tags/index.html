Day One Markdown Guide

#happenings

A general or specific account (with feelings) of events.

#explorations

Questions I ask myself that I don't have an answer to yet.

#little moments

Special moments from life - a look, a smile, a hug, a laugh.

#quotes

Great thoughts that I find along the way, from others and myself. 

#psyche

My mindset at this point in time - a snapshot of my beliefs.

#insights

Great ideas or realizations.

#thanks

What I am grateful for.

#goals

Things I want to accomplish.

#reactions

I am freaking out about something that I want to be different.

#sufferings

Moments that I am not present, worrying or looping in my head about something.

#desires

Things I believe I need but I am full of crap because I don't really need them. I capture those so I can look at letting my attachment go.

#fears

What I am afraid of and why.

#learned

Things I learned and lessons lived.

#given

How have I served others today.

Why these tags?

Because it will create a simple way for you to review back in the future. I can look at my fears, desires, and how my psyche was at the time, and see my tangible growth since.

Some ideas of what to Journal

  • Photo captions
  • Who did you meet?
  • Who did you observe?
  • What did you notice in the metro, bus, cafe or bar?
  • How do you feel? Why?
  • What did you think about in the shower?
  • Any interesting tastes, smells or sounds?
  • Specific details that define a person or place's character

Learn about yourself. Reflect on LIFE.
