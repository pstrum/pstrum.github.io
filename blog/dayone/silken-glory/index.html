Proposal

Wrapped on Silken Glory

It's crazy to think six years ago, almost to the day, was when we first kissed, isn't it? I remember when we first went upstairs to silken glory and you took my left arm and wrapped it around you. And I was hooked! You've never left my thoughts, and what I want most in the world is to work through the difficulties of life with you and be able to wrap around you at the end of each day.

Summer Text Courtship

Remember our texts and phone calls that summer? Such a unique way to be courted! And we became best friends by sharing jokes, observations, and eventually shnuggles.

Determined at Martha's Vinyard

I had to see you that first time I visited after you got back from Friday Harbor. I remember sitting on Peter Granquist's lawn on Martha's Vinyard and deciding, while talking you on the phone, that I would drive down.

Explore Each Other; Explore the World

And then I fell in love with you. We explored each other and immediately wanted to explore the world with each other. Through the past 6 years we've been to Disney, IOP, Maine, Copper Mountain, Key West, Seattle; we've become New Yorkers and Southerners. We've grown as people together. And to be honest, I feel one with you. I love you and can't even imagine life without you.

What I Want

Jennifer, I want to be one with you officially under God's eye. I want to make a reservation please: for you, forever.

The Question

Jennifer honey-hug-shnuggler leopard princess Langston - will you do me the extraordinary honor of being my wife?
